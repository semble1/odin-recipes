Lasagna

Description

This is a simple chicken taco lasagna dish that is both delicious and healthy!

A slight twist on the classic grandma's recipe that will leave you satiated yet wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Lime Juice
  • Seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, lime, and seasoning.
  2. Cook until firm, about 15 minutes.
  3. Mix cheese, sour cream, and salsa. Salt and pepper for taste.
  4. Combine everything with the noodles.
  5. Bake until tender and cheese is melted.
